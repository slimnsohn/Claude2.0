The strategic delta

One change unlocks the rest

Giving every item a unique identifier at intake (Step 3 in the ideal flow) is the foundational move. It's a small physical action — a QR sticker — but it's what makes the database, live inventory, auto-markdowns, channel routing, and learning loop possible. Without it, everything stays tribal knowledge. With it, the whole system can run.

01
Coral collapses
Human expertise concentrates into one node (specialists) instead of running through every step. Operators stop being the bottleneck.
02
Purple appears
A data layer emerges that didn't exist before. It connects pricing, inventory, and sales performance into one system.
03
Routing replaces guessing
Items go to the channel that maximizes return — floor, online, or auction — instead of all defaulting to the weekend sale.
04
Learning compounds
Every sale feeds back into the database. Next month's pricing is informed by last month's velocity. The current model has no such loop.
