What changes: Every item gets a unique ID at intake — this single move unlocks the rest. Bulk pricing becomes template pricing driven by category rules. Where Leverage Lives

The single highest-leverage move: Giving every item a unique identifier at intake. It lives in Container 3 (Integration) but it unlocks automation across all three containers. Everything downstream — inventory, markdowns, learning loops, channel routing, owner reports — becomes possible only after this foundation is in place.
