How Scoring Works

Severity
How much the title misleads vs what the rules actually say. High = retail traders would likely bet wrong. Medium = subtle but material gap. Low = minor technicality.
Priority Score
Combines severity, market liquidity, and how extreme the price is. Higher = more actionable. Markets at 90% or 10% with a mismatch matter more than 50/50 ones.
Categories
DATE_BOUNDARY = different deadline. SOURCE_SPECIFIC = resolves on one source only. CONDITIONAL_LOGIC = hidden conditions. HIDDEN_TECHNICALITY = fine print changes outcome.
Edge Direction
Which side benefits from the mismatch. YES = rules favor YES resolving more than title implies. NO = opposite. UNCLEAR = ambiguous.
priority = severity_weight × log(volume) × |price - 0.5| × position_multiplier
